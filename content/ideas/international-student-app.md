An app to guide international students through the complex process of studying in the US — before, during, and after their program.

## Problem
International students face many steps before and after arriving: visa applications, document requirements, banking, insurance, CPT/OPT/H1B, taxes, and more. There's no single resource that covers all of this well.

## App features
- Store sensitive documents and information securely
- Multilingual support
- Easy search and navigation
- Community section (Discord integration or bot)
- Checklists for each major milestone
- Curated links and resources
- Optional chatbot

## Information sections
- Before coming to the US: visa application, interviews
- Flying in: documents to carry, customs
- Flying out and returning: visa stamping, documents
- Staying in the US: banking, phone, health insurance, SSN, CPT, OPT, OPT STEM, H1B, Green Card, taxes, key documents (I-20)

## Resources
- State.gov international student guide
- US News international student steps
- Times Higher Education survival guide
