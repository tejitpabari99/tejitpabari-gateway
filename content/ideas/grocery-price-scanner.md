Compare grocery prices for a product across multiple stores by scanning its barcode. Point your phone at any grocery item and instantly see pricing at nearby stores.

## Ranking Analysis (Auto-rank)

The core barcode scanning functionality is straightforward, but the real challenge lies in acquiring and maintaining accurate, real-time pricing data across multiple grocery chains — most retailers don't expose public APIs for this, making data sourcing the primary feasibility bottleneck. Building reliable price databases, handling data freshness, and integrating location-aware store lookups makes this a moderately complex project with significant ongoing maintenance. Apps like Basket and Flipp already occupy this space, lowering originality, though the impact remains high given universal consumer interest in saving money on groceries.
