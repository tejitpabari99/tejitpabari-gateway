Several board game business ideas worth exploring.

## YouTube Channel — BoardGamesIn60
Explain a board game in 60 seconds. Group videos by genre: games for 2 people, party games, family-friendly, kids, etc. Potential for sponsorships.

## Rental Service
Home delivery board game rental. References:
- Bard & Baker (bardandbaker.com)
- CABS Gamers (cabsgamers.org)
- BoardGame2Go
- Off the Shelf Games

## Subscription Service
Monthly board game subscription box. Reference: UnboxBoardom.

## Cafe
A board game cafe. References:
- Snakes & Lattes (snakesandlattes.com)
- Reddit: "Are board game cafes profitable?"

Key question: On average, how many hours do customers sit? How much revenue from food, drinks, and games? What works better — flat entry charge or per-game?
