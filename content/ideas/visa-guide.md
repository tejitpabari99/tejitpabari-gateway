An app to figure out what visa you need when traveling to or through a country.

## Features
- Asks questions to determine your visa requirements based on nationality and destination
- Handles transit visas (traveling via a third country)
- Store passport and visa information for pre-filled context on future queries

## Ranking Analysis (Auto-rank)

The core concept is feasible but maintaining accurate, up-to-date visa data across hundreds of country pairs and transit rules is a significant ongoing data challenge — not just a build challenge. The impact is real since visa confusion is a genuine pain point that causes missed flights and travel disruptions, especially around transit visas which are poorly documented. Originality is low as tools like iVisa, Sherpa, and VisaHQ already address this space, so differentiation would need to come from UX or niche targeting.
