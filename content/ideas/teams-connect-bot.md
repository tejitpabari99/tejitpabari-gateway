A Microsoft Teams bot that tracks work and fosters team connection.

## Features
- Collects daily updates and weekly reflections that feed into a team connect summary
- Joins daily meetings and standups, recording work done by each person based on who speaks
- At each connect period, generates a full summary of work done, timelines, and achievements

## Ranking Analysis (Auto-rank)

The bot concept is feasible using Microsoft's Bot Framework and Teams API, but real-time speaker diarization during meetings adds significant complexity, making the build time substantial (likely months of work). The learning value is high given exposure to conversational AI, speech recognition, NLP summarization, and Teams integration. The idea is fairly derivative — similar tools like Otter.ai, Fireflies, and even native Teams transcription already cover much of this ground, limiting originality and differentiated impact.
