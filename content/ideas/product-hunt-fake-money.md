Product Hunt, but with fake money. Every month, users who sign up receive a fixed amount of fake currency to "invest" in apps and products they believe in. Products stay listed for a few months. The allocation of fake money shows what the community genuinely values.

## Ranking Analysis (Auto-rank)

The core mechanics are straightforward to build — user auth, currency distribution, allocation tracking — making it quite feasible, though a polished, production-ready version with anti-gaming measures and a real community would take moderate effort. The concept of using prediction-market or fake-money mechanics to surface genuine signal over clout-based upvoting is a solid insight, but similar ideas (like play-money prediction markets or weighted voting systems) have existed for a while, limiting originality. Impact depends heavily on achieving critical mass of users, which is the hardest part — the product discovery space is crowded and network effects take time to build.
