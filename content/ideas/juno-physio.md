An app for physio patients to digitize and follow their exercise plans.

## Background
When you visit a physio, you typically receive handwritten notes and a printed sheet of exercises. This app takes that input and turns it into a structured, visual plan.

## Features
- Input physio notes and exercise sheets
- Generate a structured exercise plan with visuals
- Add notes per exercise
- Supports B2B, B2B2C, and B2C models
