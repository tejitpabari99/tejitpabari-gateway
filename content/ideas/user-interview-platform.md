A platform for conducting and analyzing user interviews at scale.

## Problem
Taking notes during a user interview is distracting and causes you to miss nuances. Filling out responses afterwards means relying on imperfect memory.

## Features
- Records and transcribes the interview in real time
- Maps responses to your pre-prepared questions automatically
- Analyzes what's missing or unclear in real time
- Provides follow-up prompts during the interview to dig deeper
