An app for labeling and finding authentic cuisine.

## Reviewer criteria
- Must have lived in the relevant country/region for more than 2 years
- Must eat that cuisine regularly
- Reviews are monitored and moderated

## Model
- After 5 reviews in a month, you can view other reviews for that month for free
- Otherwise, pay to access

## Ranking Analysis (Auto-rank)

The core app mechanics are buildable, but verifying reviewer credentials (2+ years in a country) is a major unsolved challenge that undermines feasibility — there's no reliable way to enforce this without significant friction or fraud risk. Time to build is high because it requires a full review platform, moderation system, paywall/subscription logic, and the hard problem of identity/credential verification. The concept has some originality over Yelp-style apps by focusing on cultural authenticity, but the reviewer gatekeeping model may critically limit supply-side growth, and the freemium model based on contribution is a common pattern.
