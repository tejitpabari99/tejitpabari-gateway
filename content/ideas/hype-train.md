Hype your team using AI. A workplace tool that encourages recognition and gratitude.

## Features
- Tracks messages in team channels and gives stats on how many times someone has helped others
- Reminds people to thank others for their help on a periodic basis
- Starts a "Hype Train" — when one or more people start thanking others, the app prompts everyone to join in and mention something they're most thankful for about a colleague
- Prompts users every few days to start a hype train

## Ranking Analysis (Auto-rank)

This is a well-scoped idea that's feasible as a Slack/Teams bot with NLP for message tracking and AI-generated prompts — existing APIs make this buildable without major technical hurdles.
