An app to help plan weekly meals and grocery shopping.

## Problem
Deciding what to cook each week and buying the right amount of groceries is harder than it should be.

## Features
- Create your own recipes
- Choose from thousands of pre-loaded recipes (scraped from the web)
- Filter for easy, shared-ingredient recipes to minimize waste
- Add or remove ingredients freely
- Generate a weekly meal plan with a corresponding grocery list
- Filter for meals that share ingredients to reduce cost and waste
