An app to connect blood donors with blood banks and hospitals in India.

## Background
Nearly 12,000 people die every day in India due to lack of quality blood. There is a significant gap between clinical demand and supply.

## References
- eRaktKosh (Government of India blood bank portal)
- Indian Red Cross Society
- Existing apps: UBlood, Blood Bank on Google Play

## Features
- Connect donors with hospitals in real time
- Allow hospitals to request specific blood types
- Donor registration and availability tracking
