An improved Google Maps saved locations system with tags, colors, search, notes, and food item tracking. Google Maps saved locations are unintuitive — you can't search, tag, color-code, or filter them effectively. This fixes that.

## Features
- Tags and color coding for saved places
- Full-text search across saved locations
- Filter by type, distance, tags
- Add personal notes to places
- Track specific food items at restaurants
